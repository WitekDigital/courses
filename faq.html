Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our courses

1. Who can enroll in these courses?

Anyone with a keen interest in Data, AI, and Analytics. No prior experience is mandatory for beginner-level courses.

2. What is the course duration?

Courses range from 4 to 10 weeks depending on the program and include hands-on labs and projects.

3. Are these courses online?

Yes, all courses are fully online and include live hands-on sessions, videos, and assignments.

4. Do I get a certification?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course and its projects, a professional certification is provided.

5. What payment methods are accepted?

Payments can be made via Stripe (credit/debit cards) and other supported online payment methods.

6. Can I get a refund?

Refund policies vary by course. Please check the respective course page or contact us via the enquiry form.
